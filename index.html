Philosophy

How I Think.

01 —
Fail-closed. Always.
A verification system that can be bypassed is worse than no verification at all. Every primitive I build rejects when uncertain — SHA won't certify a device it can't prove, MANTIS won't trade when the AI disagrees, Nexus blocks before it executes.
02 —
Silicon to settlement.
Trust must be anchored to something that can't be faked — eFuse burn masks and vibration entropy at the hardware layer, before any on-chain state is written. The goal is identity you can audit at the silicon level.
03 —
Deterministic or nothing.
Probabilistic verification is guessing with extra steps. Every receipt is deterministic, auditable, and replay-safe. Chain-agnostic by design — OAP, Nexus, and SHA share a common attestation substrate. The chain is the execution layer, not the trust layer.
